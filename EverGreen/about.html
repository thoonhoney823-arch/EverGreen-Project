Who We Are

Meet the EverGreen Team

We are EverGreen, a group of Year 7 students from EDUSN Thingangyun Campus. Our project explores how planting more trees and increasing green spaces can help transform Yangon into a smarter, healthier, and more sustainable city. We believe that even small actions — like planting a single tree — can create a big impact when a community works together.

Through our research, we have learned about the serious environmental challenges Yangon faces today, and we have discovered practical solutions that combine nature with technology. Our goal is to share what we have learned and inspire others to take action for a greener future.

🏫

Our School

EDUSN Thingangyun Campus — a school committed to academic excellence, creativity, and developing students who care about their community and the environment.

Year 7 Project

This project is part of our Year 7 Smart City unit, where students explore how technology and sustainability can improve urban life in Myanmar's largest city.

🌿

Our Focus

Smart City and Environmental Sustainability — understanding how nature and technology work together to create cities that are healthier, greener, and more resilient.

Our Purpose

Mission, Vision & Objectives

🎯

Our Mission

To research, present, and promote practical solutions for increasing tree cover and green spaces in Yangon, demonstrating how nature-based solutions combined with smart technology can create a healthier, more livable, and more sustainable city for everyone.

🔭

Our Vision

A Yangon where every neighborhood has access to green spaces, where trees line every major street, where smart technology helps protect and maintain urban nature, and where the city's residents breathe clean air, live in cooler neighborhoods, and enjoy a thriving urban ecosystem.

📋

Our Objectives

To understand Yangon's environmental challenges, research proven green solutions from cities around the world, connect nature-based solutions with smart city technology, present our findings in a professional and engaging way, and inspire our community to take action for a greener Yangon.
