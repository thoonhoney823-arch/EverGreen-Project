EverGreen

More Trees, Smarter Yangon — a Year 7 Smart City Project exploring how planting more trees and increasing green spaces can help transform Yangon into a smarter, healthier, and more sustainable city.

🏫

EDUSN Thingangyun Campus

Our school, EDUSN Thingangyun Campus, is committed to developing students who think critically, care about their community, and take action for a better world. This project is part of our Year 7 Smart City and Environmental Sustainability unit.

💚 Thank You for Visiting

Thank you for taking the time to explore our website and learn about the EverGreen project. We believe that every person who learns about Yangon's environmental challenges and the green solutions available becomes part of the movement for change.

Whether you are a student, teacher, parent, policy maker, or simply someone who cares about Yangon's future, you can make a difference. Plant a tree. Support a community garden. Advocate for more green spaces in your neighborhood. Every action counts.

Together, we can make Yangon a smarter, greener, healthier, and more sustainable city — one tree at a time. 🌳

— The EverGreen Team: Iris, Aria, Marco & Ren
